Personalize Learning

The purpose of personalized learning is to provide the motivation needed for students to take charge of their own learning. This involves setting their own objectives in partnership with educators and working on projects that reflect their interests and learning style.

Tools for Students

A variety of tools is used in the classroom to support student learning, ranging from traditional to high-tech options. Examples include the whiteboard, index cards, posters, audience response systems (e.g. clicker technology), Google collaboration tools, 3D printing, and wikis.
